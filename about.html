About

Lawrence & Light Photography is more than just a photography business—it’s a storytelling experience. Founded on a passion for capturing life’s most authentic and spontaneous moments, we specialize in candid photography that reveals genuine emotion and personality. Whether it’s a couple laughing in the golden hour or a child’s first steps, we strive to preserve the real moments that make life beautiful.

Our team brings a professional yet personal approach to every project, ensuring that every client feels comfortable, seen, and valued. With an eye for detail and a commitment to creativity, we transform everyday memories into timeless works of art. From portraits and events to lifestyle shoots and digital prints, we offer a wide range of services tailored to meet your needs.

At Lawrence & Light, we believe that every photo should feel like a warm memory. Let us help you tell your story—beautifully, honestly, and with light.
